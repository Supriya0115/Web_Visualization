Twitter, as a social medium, can be leveraged to drive recommendations for market research and brand positioning . A dataset of 400+ influencers across various genres were studied[programmatically!] to achieve the following objectives -

  1. Track digital footprint of Influencers via Twitter Analytics

  2. Track reactions of Influencers to trending topics e.g. Social vs Entertainment

  3. Visualize the story from Twitter Data
